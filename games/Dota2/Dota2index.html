Dota2




Dota 2 is a deep multiplayer online battle arena that was published and developed by Valve and was intially released the 9th of july 2013. It is a 5v5 team clash game where each player picks a unique character. Dota 2 is a completley free to play game that can be played on Steam. Dota is the sequel to Defense of the Ancients and ever since has developed quiet the following even having its own dedicated Esports League. There are currently as of writing this on the 1st of November 2021 122 champions playable in Dota2.
