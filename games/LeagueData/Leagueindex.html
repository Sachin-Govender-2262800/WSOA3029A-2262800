League of Legends




League of Legends or know as LOL for short is one of the most popular multiplayer online battle arena otherwise known as a MOBA. It was created by Riot Games and was intially designed for Pc but has no branched to moblie devices. The game was initally released on October the 27th 2009. As of writing this in 2021 there are 157 released and playable champions in League of Legends with Vex being the most recent.
